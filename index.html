What is Insomnia

According to Mayo Clinic , Insomnia is a common sleep disorder that can make it hard to fall asleep, hard to stay asleep, or cause you to wake up too early and not be able to get back to sleep.

Causes

  • Stress
  • Travel or work schedule
  • Poor sleep habits
  • Eating too much late in the evening

Here are some things that might help with Insomnia

  • Going to bed and wake up at the same time every day
  • Relax 1 hour before bed e.g taking a bath
  • Make sure your bedroom is dark and quiet
